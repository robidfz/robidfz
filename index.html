About me

Roberta De Fazio is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mathematics and Physics at the University of Campania "Luigi Vanvitelli". She is an active member of the Smartists Research Group, where her research focuses on the development of multi-domain methods for predictive maintenance in different industrial sectors (e.g., railway and healthcare). Her interests lie in Predictive Maintenance, Process Mining and Artificial Intelligence.

Roberta earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Mathematics, as well as her Master’s Degree in Mathematics from the University of Campania Luigi Vanvitelli. As part of her Ph.D. studies, Roberta spent three months as a visiting researcher at Hasselt University in Belgium and six months at a corporate research laboratory with Gematica S.r.l., gaining valuable international and industry experience.
